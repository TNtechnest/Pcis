Curriculum

What Students Will Learn

01
Introduction to Startups
Understand what startups are, how they work, and what it takes to build one from scratch.
02
Design Thinking & Problem Solving
Learn human-centered design methods to identify real-world problems and craft creative solutions.
03
Idea Validation
Test your ideas before building — learn customer discovery and rapid validation techniques.
04
Prototype Development
Build a working model of your idea using hands-on tools and guided mentorship.
05
Business Model Canvas
Map out your entire business — from customer segments to revenue streams — in one framework.
06
Branding & Marketing
Craft a compelling brand identity and learn how to reach your target customers.
07
Financial Basics
Understand costs, pricing, and simple financial planning for a new venture.
08
Startup Pitch Training
Master the art of storytelling and pitch your startup idea to a real audience and panel.
